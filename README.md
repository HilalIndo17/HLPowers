## 📖 About

HLPowers is a data pack that gives you incredible abilities to use in your world. This pack includes modified items and player stat boosts. Enhance your combat experience with these powers!

---

## ✨ Features

- ⚔️ **Modified items** with unique abilities
- 📊 **Enhanced player stats** (health, speed, etc.)
- ✨ **Some powers** provide unique particle effects
- 🔓 **Unlock powers in two ways:** at level 30 or by using the command `/function hl_powers:power/enable`
- 📖 **The full guide** is available in-game via the command `/function hl_powers:help`
- 🌟 **And more!**
