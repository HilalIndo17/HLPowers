## 📖 About

HLPowers is a datapack that adds **permanent stat boosts** and **unique skills** – unlocked at level 30 and triggered by throwable items, item drops, or approaching mobs while holding redstone.

Enhance your combat and exploration with a full power system that feels natural and rewarding.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🔓 **Unlock all powers permanently** – Reach level 30 or run `/function hl_powers:power/enable`
- 📊 **Permanent stat boosts** – Health, resistance, saturation, and more
- ⚔️ **Multiple trigger methods**:
  - Throwable items (snowball, trident, bow, eye of ender, etc.)
  - Dropping specific items from your inventory
  - Approaching special mobs while holding redstone in your offhand
- ✨ **Unique visuals** – Every skill has its own particle effects and custom auras
- 🛡️ **Custom status effects** – Some skills apply unique effects to enemies
- 📖 **In-game guide** – `/function hl_powers:help`

---

## 🎮 How to Get Started

1. Install the datapack in your world (or server).
2. Reach level 30 **or** run `/function hl_powers:power/enable` to unlock all powers.
3. Once unlocked, trigger abilities by:
   - Throwing certain items
   - Dropping specific items
   - Approaching mobs with redstone in your offhand
4. Enjoy your permanent stat boosts and unique skills!
